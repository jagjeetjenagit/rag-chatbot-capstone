## Executive Summary

TechCorp delivered strong Q3 2025 results, exceeding revenue targets by 12% and maintaining healthy profit margins despite increased investment in R&D and talent acquisition. The company continues to see robust growth in its AI/ML product line and cloud infrastructure services.

**Key Highlights:**
- **Revenue**: $68.5M (↑15% YoY, ↑8% QoQ)
- **Net Income**: $12.3M (18% margin)
- **New Customers**: 127 enterprise clients
- **Employee Headcount**: 523 (↑45 from Q2)
- **Customer Retention**: 96.5%
- **Net Promoter Score**: 72
