# TechCorp Benefits Utilization & Company Growth Report 2025

**Report Period**: January 1 - September 30, 2025
**Prepared by**: HR Operations & Benefits Team
**Report Date**: October 1, 2025
**Classification**: Confidential - Internal Use

---

## Executive Summary

TechCorp invested $28.5M in employee benefits during Q1-Q3 2025, serving 523 employees and 847 total covered individuals. Healthcare remains the largest benefit expense at $15.2M (53%), with 401(k) matching at $8.55M (30%) and other benefits at $4.75M (17%). Employee satisfaction with benefits stands at 8.7/10, and benefits are cited as a top reason for joining in 67% of new hire surveys.

**Key Metrics**:
- Total Benefits Cost: $28.5M
- Cost per Employee: $54,494
- Participation Rate: 94%
- Satisfaction: 8.7/10
- Retention Impact: +12% vs industry
