## Executive Summary

TechCorp's total compensation budget for 2025 is $142.5M, representing a 15.2% increase from 2024. The company employs 523 people with an average salary of $156,400. Engineering remains the highest-paid department with an average of $178,200.

**Key Highlights**:
- Total Payroll: $142.5M annually
- Average Salary: $156,400
- Median Salary: $138,000
- Salary Range: $70,000 - $450,000
- Average Raise: 8.7%
- Retention Rate: 88.8%
