# TechCorp Training, Development & Learning Investment Report 2025

**Report Period**: January 1 - September 30, 2025
**Prepared by**: Learning & Development Team
**Report Date**: October 1, 2025
**Classification**: Confidential - Internal Use

---

## Executive Summary

TechCorp invested $1.5M in employee training and development during Q1-Q3 2025, with employees dedicating 20,755 hours to learning activities. The company achieved 87% training completion rates, 67% skill certification attainment, and measured a 340% ROI on training investments through productivity improvements and reduced hiring costs.

**Key Metrics**:
- Training Budget: $1.5M
- Learning Hours: 20,755 hours
- Participants: 523 employees (100%)
- Programs: 47 training initiatives
- Certifications: 189 earned
- ROI: 340%
