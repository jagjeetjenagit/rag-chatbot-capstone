**Report Period**: January 1 - September 30, 2025
**Prepared by**: HR Analytics & Operations

**Classification**: Confidential - Internal Use

## Executive Summary

TechCorp employees logged 826,400 total work hours in Q1-Q3 2025, averaging 42.3 hours per week. The company maintains a healthy work-life balance with 82% of employees working standard 40-45 hour weeks. Engineering teams show the highest productivity with 87% of time spent on value-creating activities.

**Key Metrics**:
- Total Work Hours (Q1-Q3): 826,400 hours
- Average Weekly Hours: 42.3 hours
- Overtime Hours: 4.2% of total
- Remote Work: 38% of all hours
- Meeting Time: 28% of work hours
- Productive Coding Time (Engineering): 52%
