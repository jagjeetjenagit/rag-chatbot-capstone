## Executive Summary

TechCorp allocated $150.8M across 10 departments in Q1-Q3 2025, with Engineering ($56.1M) and Sales & Marketing ($53M combined) representing 72% of total spending. The company achieved strong ROI across revenue-generating functions (Sales 450%, Marketing 799%, Product 2,204%) while maintaining efficient support operations. Overall budget utilization stands at 93%, with projected full-year spending of $201M against a $210M budget.

**Key Metrics**:
- Total Budget (Q1-Q3): $150.8M
- Budget Utilization: 93%
- Revenue Generating: $108.5M (72%)
- Support Functions: $42.3M (28%)
- Average ROI (revenue depts): 876%
