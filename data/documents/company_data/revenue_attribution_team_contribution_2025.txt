## Executive Summary

TechCorp generated $204.5M in revenue across Q1-Q3 2025, with clear attribution to team contributions: Sales directly closed $204.5M, Customer Success drove $24.6M in expansion, Engineering enabled $198M in platform value, Product influenced $189M through product-led growth, Marketing generated $142M in influenced pipeline, and Data Science contributed $67M through AI features and optimizations. This multi-touch attribution model reveals the interconnected value creation across the organization.

## Multi-Touch Attribution Model

### Revenue Attribution Framework

**Primary Touch** (Direct Closer): 40% credit
- Sales: Closes deal

**Product Touch** (Enabler): 30% credit
- Product: Features that won deal
- Engineering: Platform that enabled

**Marketing Touch** (Generator): 20% credit
- Marketing: First touch, influenced pipeline

**Customer Success Touch** (Expansion): 10% credit
- CS: Upsells, expansions, renewals

### Example: $1M Enterprise Deal

**Goldman Sachs - $4.2M Deal** (Marcus Rodriguez)

**Attribution**:
- **Sales (40%)**: $1.68M - Marcus Rodriguez closed
- **Product (30%)**: $1.26M - AI features key to deal (Jennifer Wu, David Zhang)
- **Marketing (20%)**: $840K - Webinar first touch, nurture (Lisa Anderson team)
- **Engineering (10%)**: $420K - Platform demo, security (Alex Kumar, Olivia Martinez)

**Team Credits**:
- Marcus Rodriguez (AE): $1.68M
- Jennifer Wu (VP Product): $630K (AI product)
- David Zhang (Eng): $378K (AI engine)
- Alex Kumar (Eng): $252K (platform)
- Lisa Anderson (Marketing): $420K (webinar)
- Olivia Martinez (Eng): $168K (security)
