## Executive Summary

TechCorp's 10 departments collectively delivered $204.5M in revenue with 94% employee retention and 8.4/10 satisfaction scores. Engineering leads productivity with 127 features shipped, Sales achieved 28% win rates with $680M pipeline, and Customer Success maintained industry-leading 118% net retention. All departments met or exceeded their quarterly OKRs.

**Department Highlights**:
- Highest Revenue: Sales ($204.5M direct)
- Highest ROI: Product (2,204%)
- Highest Productivity: Engineering (127 features)
- Highest Satisfaction: Data Science (9.1/10)
- Highest Retention: Customer Success (98%)
