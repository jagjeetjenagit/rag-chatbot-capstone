## Executive Summary

TechCorp delivered exceptional financial performance in the first three quarters of 2025, with revenue of $204.5M (+17% YoY) and net profit of $36.9M (18% margin). The company is on track to achieve $275M in revenue and $50M in profit for the full year.

**Key Financial Highlights**:
- **Total Revenue**: $204.5M (Q1-Q3 2025)
- **Gross Profit**: $143.2M (70% margin)
- **Operating Profit**: $36.9M (18% margin)
- **Net Profit**: $36.9M (18% margin)
- **EBITDA**: $47.6M (23% margin)
- **Cash Position**: $87.5M
- **Burn Rate**: Profitable (+$11.1M FCF/quarter)
